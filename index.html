About the Obaa Afia Foundation

At the heart of the Obaa Afia Foundation is a promise — a promise to turn personal loss into purpose. Founded in loving memory of Mrs. Janet Obeng (Obaa Afia), our mission is to bring hope, awareness, and access to health services for kidney disease and hypertension in Ghana.

Why our work matters

The Obaa Afia Foundation is focused on reducing the risk of kidney disease through early screening for hypertension and diabetes, improving community health through education and outreach, and creating opportunities for women to strengthen their role in family health and economic development.

Kidney Diseases

Kidney disease is a growing health concern in Ghana, often going undetected until it reaches advanced stages. Early diagnosis and regular checkups are key to preventing serious complications.

30%

Hypertension

Hypertension, or high blood pressure, is a silent killer that affects many Ghanaians. It often goes unnoticed until it leads to severe health issues, including kidney damage.
